Welcome to Naughty or Nice! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Child's Name: Used to personalize the Santa Claus experience
  • Parent/Guardian Email Address: Used to send copies of letters and provide customer support
  • Country: Used for localization and regional settings
  • Phone Number (Optional): Only collected if you choose to participate in giveaways

1.2 Audio Data

When you use the voice call feature:

  • We temporarily process voice recordings to enable real-time conversation with Santa Claus
  • Audio data is transmitted to Google's Gemini AI service for speech recognition and response generation
  • We do not permanently store voice recordings
  • Audio is deleted immediately after the conversation ends

1.3 Written Messages

When you write letters or chat with Santa:

  • Messages are processed to generate personalized responses
  • Messages are stored temporarily to maintain conversation context
  • Letters may be sent to the provided email address

1.4 Purchase Information

When you make in-app purchases:

  • Google Play handles all payment processing
  • We receive confirmation of purchases but do not have access to payment card details
  • Purchase history is stored locally on your device

1.5 Images

If you upload or generate images:

  • Images are processed by Google's Gemini AI for analysis and generation
  • Generated images are stored locally on your device
  • We do not permanently store uploaded images on our servers

1.6 Technical Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Language preference
  • App usage statistics (features used, session duration)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing Services: To enable conversations with Santa Claus, generate personalized letters, and deliver the core app experience
  • Communication: To send letters to your email and respond to customer support requests
  • App Improvement: To understand how users interact with the app and improve features
  • Purchase Management: To process and track in-app purchases
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

3.1 Google Gemini AI

  • Purpose: Powers all AI features including voice conversations, chat, letter generation, and image analysis
  • Data Shared: Text messages, audio recordings (temporarily), and images
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

3.2 Google Play Billing

  • Purpose: Processes in-app purchases
  • Data Shared: Purchase information
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

3.3 Vercel (Hosting)

  • Purpose: Hosts our API endpoints
  • Data Shared: API requests and responses
  • Privacy Policy: Vercel Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Local Storage: Most data (purchase history, generated content) is stored locally on your device
  • Encryption: All data transmitted between the app and our servers uses HTTPS encryption
  • Access Control: API keys and sensitive credentials are stored securely and not exposed to users
  • No Long-Term Audio Storage: Voice recordings are not stored after processing
  • Temporary Processing: Data sent to third-party AI services is processed in real-time and not permanently stored by us

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for children to enjoy under parental supervision. We comply with children's privacy regulations:

  • We require a parent/guardian email address before using key features
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental awareness
  • We do not share children's information with third parties except as necessary to provide the service (e.g., Google Gemini AI for generating responses)
  • Parents can request deletion of their child's data by contacting us

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We only share data:

  • With Google services (Gemini AI, Play Billing) as necessary to provide app functionality
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we have about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (most data is stored locally and can be deleted by uninstalling the app)
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Opt-Out: Stop using features that collect specific types of data

8. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Email Submissions: Retained as long as necessary to provide customer support
  • Audio/Images: Deleted immediately after processing
  • Purchase Records: Retained as required by Google Play and tax regulations

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States where Google's services are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or by email. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised.
